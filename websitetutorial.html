Website Tutorial

A guide on how to use our platform.

Registration

The registration process is different for students and researchers.

Students:

Students need to fill out the application form to access our platform. When the student is approved, an email will be sent with the link to the platform attached. This application consists of answering a few short response questions and the student's resume.

Professors:

Make sure to use an institutional email (usually ending in .edu). This is for us to confirm your identity.

NOTE: Make sure you use an acceptable name.

Browsing Projects

You can find projects to work on either by browsing the latest project posts or filtering through the subjects of projects that you are interested in. If you need to remove your filter selections, press the refresh button on the right.

Posting

  1. Press the "plus" button on the right to create a post.
  2. Create a project name. Generally, the more innovative or eye catching this title is, the more students will interact.
  3. Give a general description of what the project is about, the duration of the project, the time commitment you are looking for, location, and the responsibilities of the student.
  4. Choose the type of students you are looking for: high school or undergraduate.
  5. Select the field of science you best fit in. If you cannot find one similar to yours, choose "Other" and please let us know.
  6. Press "Post" to officially post the opportunity.

NOTE: Posts can be edited.

Profile

Information that can be found on the profile:

  • Name
  • Institution
  • Email
  • Resume/LinkedIn
  • Other projects by this profile
